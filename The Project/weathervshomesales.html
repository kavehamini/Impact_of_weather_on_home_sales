Impact of Weather on House Sales in King County, WA


In this project the impact of weather on the house sales as well as house prices in King County, WA has been investigated. Also the impact of different conditions of the houses on their price has been studied.

Tools Used: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Jupyter Notebook, Matplotlib


Datasets Used The house sales dataset was obtained from Kaggle. It includes the data for houses sold in 2014 and 2015 and it has different information about each property such as number of bedrooms, number of restrooms,
